# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-03-27
**App:** Gasolino
**Contact:** empastrelabs@proton.me

---

## 1. Overview

Gasolino is a fuel price comparison app for Spanish drivers. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app accesses or collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Location Data

**What:** Approximate or precise device location.
**Why:** To show fuel stations near you and sort them by distance.
**How:** Accessed only while the app is in use. Never stored remotely or shared with third parties.
**You can:** Deny location permission at any time in your device settings. The app will still work but will not show distance-based sorting.

### 2.2 User Preferences (Local Storage)

**What:** Your chosen preferred fuel type, displayed fuel types, measurement units (metric/imperial), and app theme.
**Why:** To remember your settings between sessions.
**How:** Stored locally on your device only. Never transmitted to any server.

### 2.3 Data Not Collected

We do **not** collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, etc.)
- Account or login data
- Payment information

---

## 3. Third-Party Data Sources

Gasolino displays fuel price data from the **Ministerio para la Transición Ecológica y el Reto Demográfico (MITECO)**, Government of Spain, via the public API at datos.gob.es. The app sends network requests to this API to fetch current fuel prices. No personal data is included in these requests.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following Google Firebase services, which automatically collect limited technical data to support app stability and performance. All Firebase services are governed by the [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

- **Firebase Analytics** (Google LLC) — collects app usage data, engagement metrics (e.g. session duration, screen views), and device metadata (e.g. device model, OS version, country). Data is used to understand how the app is used in aggregate.
- **Firebase Crashlytics** (Google LLC) — collects crash reports including stack traces, device state, and device metadata at the time of a crash. Used to identify and fix stability issues.
- **Firebase Performance Monitoring** (Google LLC) — collects app startup times, network request latency, and device metadata. Used to identify and fix performance issues.
- **Firebase App Distribution** (Google LLC) — used for distributing pre-release builds to testers.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Local preferences:** Stored on your device. Deleted when you uninstall the app.
- **Firebase Analytics data:** Retained by Google for up to 14 months, per Google's standard Analytics data retention policy.
- **Firebase Crashlytics reports:** Retained by Google for 90 days.
- **Firebase Performance data:** Retained by Google for 30 days.
- **Firebase App Distribution data:** Managed by Google per their policies.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- Access data held about you
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent

Since we do not collect personal data ourselves, most requests relate to Firebase services (Google LLC). For any questions, contact us at **empastrelabs@proton.me**.

Gasolino is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
